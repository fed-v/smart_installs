Corporate Social Responsability

At Smart Installs we are committed to minimizing our carbon footprint. That is why we have partnered with createyourforest.ca to plant a tree for each installation. We also properly ensure the recycling of old technology as well as provide advice on how to configure your nest products to help you save energy. That is just our way of giving back to our community.
